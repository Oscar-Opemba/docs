## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request your assessment">
    Submit your security assessment request through our contact form. Describe your scope, service type, and timeline requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scoping & kickoff">
    Our team reviews your request and schedules a scoping call to define testing boundaries, rules of engagement, and deliverables.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Testing engagement">
    Our security engineers conduct the assessment using our structured six-phase methodology. You receive updates throughout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report delivery">
    Receive a detailed vulnerability report with CVSS scores, proof-of-concept findings, and a prioritized remediation roadmap. Access it securely through the client portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All assessments are conducted under strict confidentiality. Your findings are never shared publicly and are accessible only through your authenticated client portal account.
</Note>
