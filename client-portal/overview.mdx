The Pentest Consultancy client portal gives you a single, secure location to monitor every security assessment we run on your behalf. Once your engagement is confirmed, you receive portal credentials and can immediately see a live summary of your projects, follow timeline updates as work progresses, and download delivered reports — all without emailing your engagement manager for a status update.

<Note>
  Access to the portal is restricted to authenticated users only. Your credentials are sent to you when your engagement is confirmed. If you have not received them, contact your engagement manager.
</Note>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive your portal access credentials">
    When your engagement is confirmed, you will receive an email containing your portal login credentials. Keep these credentials secure — they provide access to confidential assessment reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in at the client portal URL">
    Open the client portal URL provided in your onboarding email and sign in with the credentials you received. You must be authenticated to view any portal content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your dashboard summary">
    After signing in, your dashboard displays four key metrics: total projects, active projects, completed projects, and reports available. Use this summary to get an instant picture of where each engagement stands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to your projects and reports">
    Select any project card on the dashboard to open its detail view, where you can read the full project scope, check status, follow the timeline, and open available reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>
