## What you receive

Every engagement — regardless of service type — concludes with a structured set of deliverables designed to be useful to both technical teams and executive stakeholders.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Detailed vulnerability report" icon="file-lines">
    Each finding is documented with a title, description, reproduction steps, supporting evidence, and a CVSS score. Findings are grouped by severity — Critical, High, Medium, Low, and Informational — so your team can triage efficiently.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Risk prioritization and remediation guidance" icon="list-check">
    Vulnerabilities are ranked by exploitability and business impact. Each finding includes specific, actionable remediation steps rather than generic recommendations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Proof-of-concept demonstrations" icon="bug">
    Where appropriate, our engineers include proof-of-concept code or screenshots that demonstrate exploitability. This removes ambiguity and helps developers reproduce and verify fixes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Executive summary" icon="chart-bar">
    A non-technical summary covering overall risk posture, the most critical findings, and business impact. Written for CISOs, CTOs, and board-level stakeholders.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
