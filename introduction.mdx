## Service types

Every service type maps to a distinct attack surface. You select the service that matches your scope, or combine multiple disciplines into a single engagement.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Web application testing">
    Targets your web-facing applications for vulnerabilities from the OWASP Top 10 and beyond, including authentication flaws, injection vulnerabilities, insecure direct object references, business logic errors, and session management weaknesses. Coverage spans both unauthenticated and authenticated attack surfaces.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="API security">
    Covers REST, GraphQL, and SOAP APIs for broken authentication, excessive data exposure, improper authorization, and mass assignment issues. Testing follows the OWASP API Security Top 10 and includes both black-box and grey-box scenarios.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mobile security">
    Assesses iOS and Android applications through static analysis, dynamic runtime testing, and reverse engineering. Findings cover insecure data storage, improper certificate validation, and backend API exposure specific to mobile clients.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Network penetration testing">
    Evaluates your external perimeter and internal network segments through active exploitation and lateral movement simulation. Covers exposed services, default credentials, misconfigurations, and privilege escalation paths.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cloud security assessment">
    Reviews your AWS, Azure, or GCP environment for misconfigured IAM policies, publicly exposed storage, weak network controls, and privilege escalation risks. Assessment follows the Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) and NIST frameworks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Red team operations">
    Simulates a targeted, multi-stage adversary campaign against your people, processes, and technology. Red team engagements are goal-oriented — typically focused on Crown Jewel data or critical system compromise — and test your detection and response capabilities alongside your preventive controls.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Standards alignment

All assessments are conducted under recognized industry frameworks. This means your findings are mapped to controls your compliance and legal teams already understand.

- **OWASP** — Web Application and API Security Top 10 mapping for every relevant finding
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework** — Risk assessment findings align to NIST CSF identify and protect functions
- **PTES (Penetration Testing Execution Standard)** — Engagement structure follows PTES phases from pre-engagement through reporting

<Info>
  Pentest Consultancy holds OSCP, CEH, GPEN, and CISSP certifications across the team. Every engagement is led by a certified practitioner with demonstrated offensive security expertise.
</Info>

## Who this service is for

Pentest Consultancy works with organizations that need rigorous, professional security testing rather than automated scanning. You are a good fit if you are:

- A **CTO or engineering leader** responsible for securing a product or platform before launch or after a significant change
- A **CISO or security team** that needs external validation, a second set of eyes, or coverage your internal team cannot provide
- A **compliance officer** fulfilling PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, or other audit requirements that mandate third-party penetration testing
- A **startup or scale-up** building security credibility with enterprise customers, investors, or regulators

  Not sure which service type fits your situation? Include a description of your environment in your assessment request and the team will recommend the right scope during the initial scoping call.
</Tip>
