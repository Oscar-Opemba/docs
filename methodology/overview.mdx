Every engagement we conduct follows a structured, repeatable methodology built to give you full visibility into your security posture. Rather than ad hoc testing, our six-phase process ensures consistent coverage, defensible findings, and clear remediation guidance — so you leave every assessment with results you can act on immediately.

## Six-phase testing process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scoping & threat modeling">
    Define testing boundaries, objectives, and constraints before any active testing begins. We identify your critical assets and map potential threat scenarios directly to your business risk profile — so the assessment stays focused on what matters most to your organization.

    - Business objective alignment
    - Asset inventory and prioritization
    - Threat landscape analysis
    - Testing scope and boundaries
    - Rules of engagement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnaissance">
    Gather intelligence about the target environment through passive and active information-gathering techniques. This phase replicates the approach a real attacker would take before launching an attack, giving us an accurate picture of your external exposure.

    - Passive information gathering
    - Active scanning and enumeration
    - Technology stack identification
    - Network topology mapping
    - Service and version discovery
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vulnerability identification">
    Systematically identify security weaknesses through a combination of automated scanning and in-depth manual testing. Automated tools surface known issues quickly; manual analysis catches logic flaws, misconfigured access controls, and weaknesses that scanners miss.

    - Vulnerability scanning
    - Manual code review and analysis
    - Configuration assessment
    - Access control testing
    - Business logic analysis
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exploitation & validation">
    Validate identified vulnerabilities through controlled exploitation to confirm real-world exploitability and measure actual impact. We develop proof-of-concept demonstrations so you can see precisely what an attacker could achieve — without causing production disruption.

    - Vulnerability confirmation
    - Impact assessment
    - Proof-of-concept development
    - Lateral movement testing
    - Privilege escalation attempts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Risk analysis">
    Analyze and prioritize every confirmed vulnerability based on likelihood of exploitation, potential business impact, and your specific operating context. CVSS scores provide an industry-standard baseline; business context determines your true remediation priorities.

    - CVSS scoring
    - Business impact assessment
    - Risk prioritization
    - Remediation effort estimation
    - Compliance alignment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reporting & remediation support">
    Deliver a comprehensive findings report with actionable remediation guidance — and stay engaged through resolution. You receive both an executive summary and a detailed technical report, plus access to our team for remediation questions and verification retesting.

    - Detailed vulnerability report
    - Executive summary
    - Remediation roadmap
    - Remediation verification
    - Post-engagement support
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core principles

Every phase of our methodology is guided by four core principles that shape how we engage with your team and present our findings.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Responsible disclosure" icon="shield-halved">
    We follow responsible disclosure practices throughout every engagement. You receive advance notice of all findings, and we work collaboratively with your team on remediation before any disclosure occurs. Your data and your reputation are protected at every step.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Repeatability" icon="rotate">
    Our methodology is designed to produce consistent, comparable results across engagements. This repeatability enables trend analysis over time — so you can measure the impact of your remediation efforts and track improvements to your security posture with each successive assessment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Business context" icon="building">
    We assess every vulnerability within your specific business context, not in a vacuum. Findings are prioritized based on actual risk to your operations — a critical vulnerability in an isolated system is weighted differently than the same vulnerability in a customer-facing payment flow.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Actionable insights" icon="list-check">
    Identifying a vulnerability is only the beginning. We provide clear remediation guidance, risk prioritization, and strategic recommendations so your engineering and security teams can fix issues efficiently and systematically — without needing to interpret raw findings on their own.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
